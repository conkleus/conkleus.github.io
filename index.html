BILL CONKLE KNOWS BRAND PROTECTION

When Bill and Christina founded Conkle & Olesten, they wanted to create a small firm that was capable of fighting big battles. They were soon embroiled in intellectual property litigation with giants in the cosmetics and personal care industry and won a string of cases. They took on insurance companies which refused to defend their insureds as required by their insurance policies. Every insurance company ended up paying big money. They developed methods to create, register and enforce intellectual property at a fraction of what big law was charging. Their firm was successful in its second quarter and never looked back.
