BIG BATTLES.

Conkle & Olesten is a small Southern California law firm capable of fighting big battles. For decades, Bill and Christina have been embroiled in intellectual property litigation with giants in the cosmetics and personal care industry and they have won dozens of cases. Bill and Christina take on insurance companies which refuse to defend their insureds as required by their insurance policies. Every insurance company they have tackled has paid big money. Conkle & Olesten lawyers have developed effective methods to create, register and enforce intellectual property at a fraction of big law costs.
