BILL CONKLE FEATURED SPEAKER AT BRAND PROTECTION CONFERENCE

JANUARY 30, 2014

Bill Conkle, founder of Conkle & Olesten, was a featured speaker and panelist at the International Quality & Productivity Center's January, 2014 Anti-Counterfeiting and Brand Protection Summit in San Francisco. Bill, along with lawyer Christina Olesten and paralegal Kami LaBerge, represented C&O at the conference.

The subject of Bill's panel was Cost-Effective Brand Protection Strategies. Bill told the audience about a strategy that included using the policies of Visa and Mastercard to stop payment processing for sellers of counterfeit and illegal goods. Bill explained the litigation that led to these policies. He told the audience how to use the policies of each company and recommended that they be used.

Bill also described a streamlined litigation method which he has been using for several years to file cases against counterfeiters for as little as $1250. The low fee is made possible by the effective use of paralegals to gather information and draft portions of pleadings, and the use of a simplified method of calculating damages that counterfeits cause the company using the company's accountants in the calculation of lost profits instead of expensive experts.

Bill distributed his white paper, "Bill Conkle's Strategy Combats Diversion," to attendees in order to provide information on how their companies can prevent and control the gray market. The paper can be downloaded here.
